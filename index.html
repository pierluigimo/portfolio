Business Model Transformation

Strategy: License to Subscription

The Challenge

Transitioning a legacy customer base from perpetual licensing to a recurring Cloud/Subscription model (SaaS) without eroding short-term revenue or increasing churn on a $58M portfolio.

My Action

  • Governance Architect: Designed financial framework & P&L models for subscription shift (ARR/MRR).
  • Deal Structuring: Created scenario-based analysis shifting focus to Total Contract Value (TCV).
  • Negotiation: Partnered with Sales to close $4M+ in strategic cloud contracts.

The Results

  • +$6.9M in new corporate subscription business.
  • Churn Reduced: Dropped contract loss rate from 20% to 10%.
  • Value Increase: Achieved 15% higher average contract value.

Risk Management

Resilience: Volatile Markets (Turkey)

The Challenge

Operating a subsidiary in a hyper-inflationary environment with massive currency devaluation, creating critical FX exposure on $35M+ of annual revenue.

My Action

  • Strategic Conversion: Led project to change functional currency from Lira to USD.
  • Commercial Renegotiation: Managed high-stakes renegotiations with clients like Turkcell.
  • Compliance: Ensured full alignment with US GAAP/IFRS during transition.

The Results

  • Risk Neutralized: Completely eliminated FX exposure.
  • 100% Compliance: Passed all audits with zero issues.
  • Stability: Maintained retention during critical transition.
